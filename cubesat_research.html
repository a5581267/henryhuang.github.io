What is a CubeSat?

CubeSat (U-class spacecraft) is a type of small satellite for space research that is made up of multiples of 10 cm × 10 cm × 11.35 cm cubic units.

CubeSat Subsystem

Bus & Payload

OBDH

(Onboard Data Handling Subsystem)

Brain in a CubeSat

OBDH includes the OBC board hardware and onboard flight software (FSW). In a CubeSat system, OBDH is a critical subsystem because of its responsibility of managing system and monitoring whole CubeSat.

The FSW is in charging of managing resource, monitoring system, processing telecommand and telemetry, handling data interface and scheduling time course of the mission.

In our CubeSat project, ISIS Onboard Computer (iOBC) is used as OBC to develop FSW and communicate with other subsystems and payloads.

RTOS

Our FSW is developed based on FreeRTOS. FreeRTOS is a RTOS kernel to develop embedded application and has been ported to 35 microcontrollers platforms.

To achieve different functions, RTOS-tasks are designed by developer and controlled by FreeRTOS-scheduler.
